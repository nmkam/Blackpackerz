Who are we? This is our story!

Hello and welcome to our homepage! I'm Eb from Nantwich and I'm Nick from Luton. In August 2018 we decided that we wanted to travel the world together with our first goal being to complete a Working Holiday Visa (WHV) in Australia so we started saving and working towards our dream and just over a year later on September 1st 2019, we set off from Heathrow Airport bound for Bangkok where we spent 2 months together in Thailand, travelling from Kanchanaburri, to Koh Tao, to Koh Phangnan and finally to Chiang Mai.


At the end of October, I left Eb behind in Chiang Mai and headed to Perth in Western Australia. Eb joined me at the end of November and after buying a car, we set off on our Aussie journey, travelling and sight-seeing up and down the west coast!
